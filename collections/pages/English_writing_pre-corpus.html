Key Points for Cover Letter

  • Why is this topic important? （研究重要性）

  • What is the key result? （主要结果）

  • What is the significance of your result? （结果的意义）

  • How does it advance the field? （结果对领域的发展）

  • Why does your paper fit this journal? （为什么选择该期刊）

  • Do NOT copy the Abstract （不要复制摘要）

  • Do NOT oversell （不要过渡推销）

  • Any disclosures?(conflicts of interest, relatedunpublished papers, prior submission, reviewers etc.）

  • Keep it succinct! （保持简洁）

一个Cover Letter例子

The Abstract - Keep it Simple!

  • Reason and Importance of Research

  • Simple Introduction to Procedure

  • Brief Description of the Experiment

  • Overview of the Results

  • Familar keywords, Omit Abbreviations

    7-10 sentences- No citations- No abbreviations- Findings first
